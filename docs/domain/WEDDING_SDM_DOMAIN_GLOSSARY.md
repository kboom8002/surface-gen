# Wedding SDM Domain Glossary

## 1. Industry Type

```yaml
industry_type: wedding_sdm
```

`wedding_sdm` covers Korean wedding service brands related to studio, dress, makeup, wedding day snap, and venue/hall consultation contexts.

## 2. Core Service Domains

### Studio
Pre-wedding or rehearsal photography, indoor/outdoor studio shoots, editorial shoots, natural light shoots, and album delivery.

### Dress
Dress fitting, dress rental, designer profiles, silhouette guidance, and dress styling support.

### Makeup
Bride/groom makeup, hair styling, rehearsal makeup, wedding day makeup, and skin/hair preparation guidance.

### Snap
Wedding day photography, ceremony/banquet coverage, dark-hall shooting considerations, and delivery/editing policy.

### Venue
Wedding hall, ceremony space, banquet flow, aisle, lighting, parking, and guest movement contexts.

### SDM Package
Integrated studio-dress-makeup package, often bundled or recommended through consultation.

## 5. Visual Concepts

### Actual Photo
A real uploaded or client-provided image that may represent actual work only if rights and review status allow public use.

### AI Visual
A generated or synthetic visual used only for editorial explanation, process explanation, package concept support, or internal moodboarding. It must never represent actual work.

### Portfolio Docent
A high-density explanatory asset that interprets an album or visual set for customer decision-making.

### Style Collection
A visual grouping of portfolio and gallery items by season, mood, vibe vector, or style direction.

### Vibe Vector
A numerical representation of visual mood across axes such as bright-to-moody, natural-to-editorial, relaxed-to-formal, minimal-to-luxury, intimate-to-grand, and documentary-to-directed.

## 7. Policy Terms

### Reservation Deposit
Initial payment or deposit condition for booking.

### Cancellation and Refund
Conditions for cancellation, refund availability, refund limits, and timing.

### Schedule Change
Rules for date changes, weather-related changes, and availability.

### Delivery
Timeline and format for originals, edited images, albums, files, or prints.

### Copyright and Portrait Rights
Usage rights, customer consent, portfolio publication permission, and restrictions.

### Additional Fee
Extra charges for retouching, time extension, location, weekend/peak season, album upgrade, assistant, or external vendors.

## 8. SEO/AEO/GEO Terms

### SEO
Search engine optimization for discoverability and ranking.

### AEO
Answer engine optimization: structuring content so direct answers can be extracted.

### GEO
Generative engine optimization: making brand entities, claims, proofs, and answer surfaces clear for generative AI systems.

### QIS
Question Intent Scene: a canonical customer question mapped to intent, funnel stage, asset type, proof, and next action.

### Brand Knowledge Graph
A structured graph connecting brand, services, programs, visual assets, styles, audiences, problems, answers, policies, proofs, and CTAs.
