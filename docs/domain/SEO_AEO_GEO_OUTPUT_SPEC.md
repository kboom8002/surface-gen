# SEO/AEO/GEO Output Specification

## 1. Purpose

This document defines the SEO, AEO, and GEO outputs generated by the Wedding Surface Agent.

SEO means search-engine discoverability and crawlable content quality.
AEO means answer-engine optimization: the ability for generated content to answer real customer questions directly.
GEO means generative-engine optimization: the ability for AI systems to understand the brand, cite the right claims, and avoid distorted summaries.

The system must not generate keyword-stuffed content. All outputs must support real customer decisions and must be grounded in the Brand Factsheet, verified service data, visible photo facts, and approved evidence.

## 2. Canonical Files

The advanced semantic layer writes these files under `02_semantic_strategy/`:

```text
search_intent_map.json
qis_growth_registry.json
entity_keyword_map.json
local_geo_intent_map.json
ai_answer_opportunity_map.json
```

These files are not direct CMS upload files, but they guide UCA generation, internal linking, schema readiness, answer generation, growth loops, and monthly patch cycles.

## 8. Generation Rules

- Prioritize customer decision scenes over generic keyword volume.
- Do not generate unsupported local venue names.
- Do not create exact pricing keywords unless pricing is confirmed.
- Use customer language, not internal taxonomy labels.
- Every high-risk commercial or policy query must link to a policy card.
- Every visual-style query should link to portfolio_docent or style_collection.
- Every action query should link to contact_info, match_brief, or cta_block.

## 9. Validation Rules

The semantic strategy output passes only when:

- At least 30 QIS records exist for v1.
- At least 6 intent families are represented.
- Every QIS record has a target asset type.
- Every commercial/risk QIS has related policy IDs or a review_required flag.
- No forbidden public UCA type is referenced.
- Local entities are marked verified or review_required.
